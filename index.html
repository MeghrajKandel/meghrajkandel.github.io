About Me

I am a researcher in artificial intelligence and data science, focusing on real-time tourism demand forecasting. My work centers on the ATLAS framework (Adaptive Tourism LLM with Aligned Signals), an AI-driven system that leverages large language models and aligned data streams to improve forecasting accuracy. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science & IT (Lumbini ICT Campus) and an M.Sc. in Networking (Melbourne Institute of Technology), and I am currently pursuing a Master of ICT (Research).
